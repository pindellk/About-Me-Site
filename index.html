About Me

Hi! My name is Katie. I currently live in Washington, D.C., and for the past 5 years I've worked in the federal financial consulting field doing financial reporting and analysis. I'm currently enrolled in a coding boot camp in order to challenge myself by learning a new set of skills. I grew up in Howard County, Maryland and attended the University of North Carolina at Asheville for undergrad. I graduated in May, 2015 with joint degrees in Accounting and Management.

In my free time I love hiking and camping and try to explore a few new National Parks each year. I also like making music and taking pictures, so I'm excited to use my new skills to share my creative side - check out my portfolio page to see what I've made so far!
